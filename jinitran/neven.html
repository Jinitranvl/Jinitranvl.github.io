Neven

Neven is a small winery and spirits maker based in Serbia but sources local ingredients from all countries in the former Yougoslavia. Small batch productions with bottles only ever being given away as gifts or samples to friends and family, Neven is a hobby-based project given life by a retired volleyball coach from Bosnia.
